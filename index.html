Privacy Policy for Just Sky

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Just Sky is a free mobile weather application developed by Anton Sobolevskyi ("we", "us", "Service Provider"), based in Lviv, Ukraine.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how Just Sky handles information.

Information Accessed

The app requests permission to access your device's location (approximate and/or precise) solely to provide accurate local weather forecasts.

  • Location data is processed only on your device to determine coordinates or prepare a weather query.
  • It is not stored by the app or sent to our servers.
  • To fetch weather data, the app may send the location (e.g., latitude/longitude or city name) in a request to OpenWeatherMap API.
  • OpenWeatherMap does not collect, store, or retain location data or other request parameters (per their Privacy Policy). It is used only ephemerally to generate and return the forecast.

We do not collect, store, or share any other personal information, including:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Device identifiers (beyond basic OS requirements for app operation)
  • IP address, usage analytics, browsing history, or advertising data
  • No AI processing, no marketing communications, no user accounts

Third-Party Services

The only external service is OpenWeatherMap for weather data. See their privacy policy for details: https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy. We do not use analytics, ads, crash reporting, or other SDKs that collect data.

Your Rights and Controls

  • You can grant, deny, or revoke location permission anytime in your device settings.
  • If location is denied, the app may allow manual city entry (if you implement this feature).
  • Uninstalling the app stops all access to device data.

Data Retention and Security

No personal or location data is retained on servers. The app operates with basic security measures provided by the Android platform.

Children

Just Sky is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
